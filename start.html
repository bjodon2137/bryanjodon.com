The process

What to expect on a first call.

The intro call is 30 minutes. No pitch, no pressure. Here's what usually happens.

  • 01

    You tell me what's going on

    What communication challenge are you trying to solve? Is it a specific relationship? A pattern you keep hitting? A team dynamic that's slowing things down? I want to understand the actual situation before I suggest anything.

  • 02

    I share what I'm hearing

    Usually I can identify the style dynamic pretty quickly and name what's happening. Often people find this valuable on its own — just having a framework for the friction they've been experiencing without a language for it.

  • 03

    We figure out next steps

    If coaching or a workshop makes sense, we'll talk through what that looks like. If the free guide is all you need, I'll tell you that too. The goal is to figure out the right answer for your situation, not to sell you something.
