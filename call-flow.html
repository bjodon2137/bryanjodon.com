Reading the Room
Listen for these in the first 30 seconds of the call.
D Dominant — Red Flags
"Just tell me what to do" or cuts you off mid-sentence
Impatient with hold times; restates the problem forcefully
Asks "how long is this going to take?" within the first 30 seconds
Tone is clipped, no small talk, speaking fast
I Influential — Gold Flags
Jokes, laughs, or makes conversation before stating the issue
Uses phrases like "I know you guys are busy" — empathetic opener
Expresses frustration emotionally: "This is so annoying, honestly"
Responsive to your name, uses yours back
S Steady — Green Flags
Apologizes for calling or says "I didn't want to bother anyone"
Speaks quietly, hesitates before asking questions
Asks "is this going to mess anything else up?" before acting
Very compliant, follows steps exactly, confirms each one
C Conscientious — Blue Flags
Describes the problem with timestamps and detail: "It started Tuesday at 4pm"
Asks "why" before doing anything: "Why would unplugging it help?"
Has already tried things and reports results accurately
Skeptical or corrects you if you use a term imprecisely
